Whisker – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-06-02

Whisker does not collect, store, or share personal data. The app:

  • Uses TikTok OAuth solely to obtain an access token for the owner’s account.
  • Uploads video files generated locally; no user information is transmitted.
  • Stores tokens only on the owner’s private server (n8n).
